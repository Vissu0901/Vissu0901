Library Management System

A library, which may vary widely in size, may be organized for use and maintained by a public body
such as a government; an institution such as a school or museum; a corporation; or a private individual. In addition to
providing materials, libraries also provide the services of librarians who are trained and experts at finding, selecting, circulating
and organizing information and at interpreting information needs, navigating and analyzing very large
amounts of information with a variety of resources.
